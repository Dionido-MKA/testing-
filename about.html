About Us

DadBod is a hamburger restaurant located at 67 Hermana Fausta St, Lucena, 4301 Quezon, Philippines. The eatery boasts a menu filled with huge, affordable burgers and delicious beverages to complement. Customers can expect great customer service and quick takeaway service, as well as the option to dine in. The restaurant's constant flow of customers is a testament to its value/quality of food. The simple buns and well-cooked patties are paired with a variety of sauces that diners rave about. In addition to their burgers, Cheesy Fries are also a must-try. The restaurant is open from Wednesday to Tuesday, offering extended hours on weekends. Customers looking for a Shake Shack experience in Lucena will find it at DadBod.

Frequently Asked Questions

· What are your operating hours?

We are open from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday to Sunday.

· Does Dadbod accept dine-in customers?

Yes, Dadbod accepts dine-in customers, although seating may be limited during busy hours.

· Do you offer delivery or takeout services?

Yes, we offer both delivery and takeout services. You can place your order online or by phone.

· What types of beverages does the venue offer?

The venue offers a variety of beverages including refreshing options like Fresh Calamansi Lemonade and unique flavors such as Yakulade.

· Are there vegetarian options available at the venue?

No, it appears that the menu primarily features meat-based items, with limited vegetarian options currently offered.
